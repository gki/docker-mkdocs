Tilda is one of the PyMdown Extensions to provide strike through text and subscript with `~`.

## Strike through
    ~~Expected~~ Expecto patronum!

~~Expected~~ Expecto patronum!

## Subscript
    Water is H~2~O

Water is H~2~O
